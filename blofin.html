How to Use BloFin Trading on This Site

1
Go to BloFin.com and log into your account.
2
Navigate to the API Management section under your profile or settings menu.
3
Create a new API key and label it "TradeWithJars Site Access." Copy both the API Key and Secret Key.
4
Paste your keys into the boxes above.
5
Click the connect button to begin secure trading.
6
Once connected, you'll see your account and can place or monitor trades.
7
Reminder: Keep your secret key private. This site does not store your keys.
